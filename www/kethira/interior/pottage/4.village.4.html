Outbuilding: This daub and wattle building is used as sleeping quarters for visitors. Usually staying here are Sir Maild's traveling companions, Sir Yoreth and Sorakka the Invinian. Sir Yoreth is Sir Maild's personal priest and sleeps in the curtained off bed with the superior locked chest. Sorakka sleeps in the other large bed which has a distinct odour of vomit. The extra bunks are usually vacant.
