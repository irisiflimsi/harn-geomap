Cottar
(Largo of Telya)

Size: 2

Acres: 6 serf

Largo is a small unattractive 16 year old young man with red hair and a rat-like face. He has no illusions about his appearance and knows if he is to get a wife he must prove himself a good provider. Hard working with a good sense of humour has endeared him to the older women of the village but so far has been below the notice of the few girls of marrying age. He has cared for his near deaf mother since the disappearance of his father two years ago.
