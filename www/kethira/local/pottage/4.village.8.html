Half-Villein
(Drod of Fulkne)

Size: 4

Acres: 20 serf

Drod has brown eyes so dark they appear black giving him an unsettling appearance. He is cousin to Yole [14] and is married to the daughter of the reeve. They have two children, a six year old son and daughter, who's three. Recently this family has begun to experience nightmares with the children waking in tears, Drod has much anger towards the lord for abandoning his duties.
