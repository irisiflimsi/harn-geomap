Villein
(Shoral of Hintiem)

Size: 5

Acres: 30 serf

Shoral was adopted into clan Hintiem as a child; he now counts Dynis [7] and Galeman, the beadle as brothers. His wife died years ago but has four surviving children between 6 and 16 years. Shoral's 14 year old daughter has gotten herself pregnant but cannot or will not say who the father might be, many suspect Sorakka at the manor.
