Half-Villein
(Dane of Chetrik)

Size: 4

Acres: 15 serf

Four years ago Dane accidentally killed his wife by dropping a mallet from the roof on her head, fracturing her skull. He is still racked with guilt and father Habus often visits Dane to administer spiritual comfort. Dane has three children who still live with him. His 14 year old daughter is betrothed to the eldest son of the beadle [4] and is to be married next month. Dane and his sons are not looking forward to the marriage as none of them are skilled in cooking. Dane is looking forward to this years' festival, hoping to land a new wife. The household has begun to have nightmares with the children waking up screaming.
