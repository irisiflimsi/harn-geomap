Half-Villein
(Kalas of Dirth)

Size: 6

Acres: 18 serf

Kalas is a drunkard and a chronic singer, much to the chagrin of everyone around him. Friends and family have tried to convince Kalas that he can not carry a tune but this has not dampened his enthusiasm for song. His three children sing as well but are not tone deaf like their father. His tolerant wife and her older spinster of a sister round out the household.
