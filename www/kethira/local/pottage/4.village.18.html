Half-Villein
(Kjordi of Metiendor)

Size: 5

Acres: 20 serf

Kjordi lost two fingers in his youth to a wolf attack and as such, has developed a phobia towards canines. Kjordi will flee the beadle when he approaches with Majesty at his side, which is always. Kjordi's wife, two children and brother-in-law fill out his homestead.
