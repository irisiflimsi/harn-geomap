Villein
(Lamwyne of Storele)

Size: 9

Acres: 30 serf

Lamwyne and his voluptuous wife Etty, have the largest family in the village with seven healthy children. Although Lamwyne is overworked and tired he just can't leave his wife alone and she is pregnant yet again. His brother [2] helps him as much as he can but Lamwyne is looking forward to his sons being strong enough to help in the fields. At present his oldest son is only 10 and little help. The entire family has begun having disturbing dreams which they cannot remember.
