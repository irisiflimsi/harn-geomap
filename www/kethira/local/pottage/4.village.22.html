Villein - Reeve
(Konaras of Metiendor)

Size: 8

Acres: 30 serf/10 free

Fifty-two-year-old Konaras is still able to work his 40 acres with ease with the help of his eldest son. He has been reeve of the village for 23 years and is respected by all. He and his wife take care of two adopted orphans in addition to their son, daughter-in-law and two grandchildren. His second son is a guard at the manor house. He is angry with what is happening in the village as the lord is not here to defend his people and worse, he does not understand what is going on.
