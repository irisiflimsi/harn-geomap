Villein - Beadle
(Galeman of Hintiem)

Size: 5

Acres: 30 serf

Galeman has been the village beadle for five years and is well liked by the manor lord. The beadle carries a stout staff with him at all times, more than a few villagers have felt it across their backs. He is the head of his clan and a rigid man, disliking slackers. His wife and three children complete this household; his oldest son is set to marry the daughter of Dane of Chetrik [6]. Galeman has a very intelligent Kaldorin (sheepdog) named Majesty who is his constant companion. Many remark that the dog is smarter than most villagers, probably true.
