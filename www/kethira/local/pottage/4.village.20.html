Half-Villein
(Harlarn of Dirth)

Size: 6

Acres: 12 serf

Harlarn is head of a healthy family of six which includes his wife and four children between 4 and 13 years. He works for other villagers in order to feed his family as his land does not produce enough food. Harlarn has requested more land from the lord but has yet to receive a response. His cousin Kalas [11] is his best friend and drinking partner, although he does not join his cousin in song.
