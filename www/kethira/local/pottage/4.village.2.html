Half-Villein
(Sarathid of Storele)

Size: 4

Acres: 15 serf

Sarathid is a widower having lost his wife to pneumonia last winter. His two young children are watched by his mother who lives with them. Sarathid helps out his brother Lamwyne frequently [16] but is beginning to get a little resentful, feeling maybe Lamwyne should control his lust a bit more.
