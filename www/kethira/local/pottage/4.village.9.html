Half-Villein
(Bjoun of Ikren)

Size: 6

Acres: 20 serf

Bjoun is a fine farmer who received five extra acres last year as restitution for the rape of his 13 year old daughter by Simadar of Fulkne [13]. Although he sees the matter as settled he has an understandable disliking for Simadar and the reeve makes it a point to keep the men apart. Bjoun and his wife have three strong children and also take care of his elderly grandmother who is 71 this year, making her the oldest person in the fief.
