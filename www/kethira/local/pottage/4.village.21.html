Miller
(Raena of Ekenasta)

Size: 3/6*

Acres: 20 free

Quality: **

Price: Average

Raena inherited the franchise from his father and now runs it with the help of an apprentice and one journeyman. The mill is oxen powered due to the stream's poor current but there are plans to dam it and build a new mill in the future. Raena's pregnant wife bakes bread in a large oven for the locals with the help of her daughters, ages 8 and 10. Raena keeps 10% of the flour he grinds which he sells to the manor lord, who in turn sells most of it in a larger town. He is nervous for his family with all the goings on in village and is considering going to see a cousin in the north for a long visit.

(*There are 3 people involved in the milling and a total of 6 in the household)
