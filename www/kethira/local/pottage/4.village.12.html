Villein
(Jerald of Gwetrenal)

Size: 5

Acres: 25 serf

Jerald is the village prankster; sadly for the villagers he is an exceptional one. On one occasion he waited two hours dressed as a scarecrow in the fields until the reeve passed by to scare him. His wife is mortified by her husband's antics and feeds him cold pottage after every prank but this is just a price he pays for his art. When their mother is not around his three children love to share in their father's jokes and encourage him.
