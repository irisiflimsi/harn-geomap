Villein
(Dynis of Hintiem)

Size: 5

Acres: 25 serf

Dynis has walked with a slight limp since childhood when an ox crushed his foot. He and his brother the beadle look so much alike that many mistake them for each other. His youngest son was killed last year by a bear which was then slain by the manor lord. Dynis has the bear pelt on his wall as a trophy despite the objections of his wife. He has three surviving children ages seven to fourteen. The family has been having bad dreams for days.
