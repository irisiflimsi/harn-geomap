Villein
(Yole of Fulkne)

Size: 4

Acres: 26 serf

Yole is the Fulkne clan head and a talented whittler. His carved animals make it into the hands of all village children, many have more than one. He remarried last year to a girl 15 years his junior and has a baby on the way. Also in his household are two older children from his first wife, a son 14 and a daughter 11. The whole family is exhausted from the lack of sleep because of bad dreams.
