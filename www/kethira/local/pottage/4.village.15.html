Cottar
(Cella of Ikren)

Size: 2

Acres: 5 serf

Cella is sister to Bjoun [9] and a widow, having lost her husband this past winter. She and her 13 year old son are surviving but she is looking to remarry. Shoral of Hintiem [10] has begun visiting Cella which has become popular gossip amongst village folk. She experienced her first bad dream last night. Her son, who is blessed with a high aura also had a disturbing dream but remembers some of it and will share this with the PCs if asked. He recalls a beautiful maiden unable to move who is trapped in darkness and cold.
