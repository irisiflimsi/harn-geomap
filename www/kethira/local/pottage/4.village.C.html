Church of Peoni

Lady Erane is a devout Peonian and convinced her husband to construct a new cottage and tithe barn for the Peonian church. The ebasethe (priest), father Habus, holds services at an outdoor shrine or in the barn on days with poor weather. A small graveyard is present on church land as well as a tiny orchard. In addition the church holds 60 acres of fields tended by the villagers. There are plans to construct a small temple in the village but funds are lacking at the present time or so Sir Maild says.
