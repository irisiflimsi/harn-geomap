Charcoaler
(Awal of Meritta)

Size: 5/8*

Quality: ***

Prices: Average

Acres: 20 free

Awal first came to the village in 714 with his wife and two children. They are still considered outsiders to the community but are making inroads with most. In the last two years Awal has taken on two apprentices and two journeymen. Even with the added help Awal is terribly busy but his profits are way up so he has started giving a little spending money to his apprentices. His eldest child is about to be apprenticed to a master a few settlements away and his wife is distraught over this. Awal often travels to local towns with wagon loads of charcoal, he happily returns with certain goods for the other villagers. Harlarn [20] works half of Awal's land. Other locals work the other half for a portion of the harvest for themselves.
